[Banners](https://www.mongodb.design/component/banner/guidelines/) look
similar to callouts but serve a different use case. Callouts should be used
when you want to call out information to the user. Unlike banners, callouts
cannot be dismissed. They’re optimized for long form copy (banners are
optimized to save space).

Note callouts show something that “may be nice to know”.
