Banners look similar to [Callouts](https://www.mongodb.design/component/callout/guidelines/) but serve
a different use case. They are usually contextual to the product or task. If
you are looking for a way to communicate a global message, like “you are
offline” consider using a [Toast](https://www.mongodb.design/component/toast/guidelines/) .

Below is useful table to help you choose when to pick which pattern.

Limit banner usage so that there's only 1 banner displayed at a time on
screen.

The blue note banners means “this is something that may be nice to know.”
Banners can be dismiss-able (X on the top right), or they may be persistent
(no X on the top right). Consider your use case and whether it needs to be
dismissable.

Links are underlined and set to medium weight to ensure legibility on colored
backgrounds.

We recommend avoiding CTAs within banners. However, if your specific use-case
calls for it we suggest using the default color button in the extra-small size
and not having any other buttons in the banner.
