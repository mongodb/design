Our SideNav component should be used for any area of our product that has a
navigational structure.

In general, we recommend not including decorative or functional icons (that
trigger an action) in SideNav items.

[Learn more about icons.](https://www.mongodb.design/component/icon/guidelines/)

  imageURL={`${CDN}/images/new-guidelines/sideNav/sideNav-guidelines-2@3x.png`}

Avoid including cards or marketing content in the SideNav. Opt for more
contextual places within the experience to introduce the content.
