Tooltips should be used to show supplementary information only. They can be
triggered when the user hovers over an element, say an info sprinkle or
button.

  imageURL={`${CDN}/images/new-guidelines/tooltips/tooltip-guidelines-1@3x.png`}

For screens, or lists that would require many info sprinkles, consider using
the [inline definition pattern](https://www.mongodb.design/component/inline-definition/example/).

Never nest call to actions within tooltips. The behavior of moussing over
something and then mousing over a link is unexpected and doesn’t meet
accessibility best practices. (You’re hiding the link). Instead, opt for
showing the link somewhere else on the UI.
