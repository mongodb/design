Caret icons are used in select inputs or standalone menu triggers. They always
point down, and always open a new dropdown.

Chevrons are used as an affordance for a collapsible/expandable, accordion,
element. They do no open a new dropdown, but resize the existing container.
Always point the chevron in the direction of the content when open, and to the
opposite of its content when closed.

## Accessibility Requirements

Whether working in light or dark mode, ensure your icons are accessible and pass
a 3:1 contrast ratio with their background. You can check this contrast ratio by
using the [Stark plugin in Figma](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/732603254453395948/Stark).
(Gray Base is the lightest you can go in light mode for our gray palette.)
